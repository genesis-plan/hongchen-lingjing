## 红尘灵境 / Hongchen Lingjing 行为准则
### Code of Conduct for Hongchen Lingjing
**版本 / Version**：v1.0  
**生效日期 / Effective Date**：2026-01-21  
**适用范围 / Scope**：所有参与「红尘灵境」项目的贡献者、学习者、传承者、社区成员，涵盖项目所有协作空间（GitHub仓库、Discord、GitHub Discussions、线上会议等）。

This Code of Conduct applies to all contributors, learners, inheritors and community members participating in the Hongchen Lingjing project, covering all project collaboration spaces (GitHub Repository, Discord, GitHub Discussions, online meetings, etc.).

---

## 核心宗旨 / Core Purpose
「红尘灵境」以**技能传承、全球共创、开源共享**为核心初心，致力于打造包容、友好、专业的全球协作社区。本行为准则旨在建立共同的行为底线，保障所有社区成员的参与体验，杜绝歧视、冲突与不文明行为，让不同背景、不同技术水平、不同文化的成员都能在社区中平等协作、共同成长，同时兼顾项目开源生态发展与后续商业化运营的合规性。

Hongchen Lingjing takes **skill inheritance, global co-creation, open-source sharing** as its core original intention, and is committed to building an inclusive, friendly and professional global collaborative community. This Code of Conduct aims to establish a common behavioral bottom line, protect the participation experience of all community members, eliminate discrimination, conflicts and uncivilized behaviors, enable members of different backgrounds, technical levels and cultures to collaborate equally and grow together in the community, and take into account the compliance of the project's open-source ecological development and subsequent commercial operation.

---

## 我们倡导的行为 / We Encourage
1. **互相尊重与包容**：尊重不同的文化、观点、技术水平与贡献方式，认可每一份贡献的价值，无论贡献大小、技术高低。
   Respect each other and embrace inclusiveness: Respect different cultures, viewpoints, technical levels and contribution methods, and recognize the value of every contribution, regardless of its size or the contributor's technical ability.
2. **专注价值与初心**：所有交流与协作均围绕「技能传承」与项目发展展开，聚焦问题解决，拒绝无意义的争论与话题偏离。
   Focus on value and original intention: All communication and collaboration are centered on **skill inheritance** and project development, focusing on problem-solving, and rejecting meaningless arguments and off-topic discussions.
3. **开放沟通与协作**：沟通时保持清晰、友善、专业，积极倾听他人意见，乐于分享知识与经验，主动帮助新手成员融入社区。
   Open communication and collaboration: Communicate clearly, friendly and professionally, actively listen to others' opinions, be willing to share knowledge and experience, and take the initiative to help new members integrate into the community.
4. **诚信与负责**：提交的代码、文档、技能内容需保证真实、合规，尊重知识产权；对自己的贡献负责，及时跟进PR/Issue的反馈与修改。
   Integrity and accountability: The submitted code, documents and skill content must be true and compliant, and respect intellectual property rights; be responsible for one's own contributions, and follow up on PR/Issue feedback and revisions in a timely manner.
5. **勇于探索与包容试错**：鼓励技术探索、创意尝试，包容合理范围内的试错，对失误保持宽容，共同探讨解决方案而非指责。
   Courage to explore and tolerate trial and error: Encourage technical exploration and creative attempts, tolerate trial and error within a reasonable range, be tolerant of mistakes, and jointly explore solutions rather than blame.
6. **尊重社区治理与共识**：遵守社区的治理规则、贡献流程与决策结果，对争议问题通过合理渠道沟通解决，尊重社区集体共识。
   Respect community governance and consensus: Abide by the community's governance rules, contribution processes and decision results, resolve controversial issues through reasonable channels, and respect the collective consensus of the community.

---

## 我们禁止的行为 / We Prohibit
1. **歧视与不尊重行为**：基于性别、年龄、种族、国籍、宗教、性取向、技术水平、身体状况等进行歧视、嘲讽、侮辱或人身攻击。
   Discrimination and disrespect: Discrimination, mockery, insult or personal attack based on gender, age, race, nationality, religion, sexual orientation, technical level, physical condition, etc.
2. **骚扰与恶意行为**：任何形式的言语骚扰、私信骚扰、恶意刷屏、人身威胁；故意发布冒犯性、低俗、暴力、色情等不良内容。
   Harassment and malicious behavior: Any form of verbal harassment, private message harassment, malicious spamming, personal threat; intentionally publishing offensive, vulgar, violent, pornographic and other inappropriate content.
3. **知识产权侵权**：抄袭、盗用他人的代码、文档、设计作品、技能内容等，未获得授权擅自使用他人的知识产权成果。
   Intellectual property infringement: Plagiarizing or stealing others' code, documents, design works, skill content, etc., and using others' intellectual property achievements without authorization.
4. **滥用社区权限与资源**：恶意提交虚假PR/Issue、篡改仓库内容、滥用社区沟通渠道进行商业推广（非项目合作类）、盗用社区品牌与标识。
   Abuse of community rights and resources: Maliciously submitting fake PR/Issues, tampering with repository content, abusing community communication channels for commercial promotion (non-project cooperation), stealing community brands and logos.
5. **破坏社区协作氛围**：故意挑起争端、散布谣言、恶意抹黑项目或社区成员；拉帮结派，制造社区分裂；无视维护者提醒，多次违反本准则。
   Undermining the community collaboration atmosphere: Intentionally provoking disputes, spreading rumors, maliciously smearing the project or community members; cliquing and causing community division; ignoring reminders from maintainers and violating this Code multiple times.
6. **违规商业行为**：在社区内进行与项目无关的商业炒作、非法集资、虚拟货币投机等行为；未经社区共识，利用项目资源进行个人商业盈利。
   Illegal commercial behavior: Conducting irrelevant commercial speculation, illegal fund-raising, virtual currency speculation and other behaviors in the community; using project resources for personal commercial profit without community consensus.

## 社区责任 / Community Responsibilities
1. **所有成员的责任**：自觉遵守本行为准则，主动维护社区的友好氛围，对违反准则的行为进行善意提醒，或向维护团队举报。
   Responsibility of all members: Consciously abide by this Code of Conduct, take the initiative to maintain the friendly atmosphere of the community, give kind reminders to behaviors that violate the Code, or report to the maintenance team.
2. **贡献者与维护者的责任**：不仅要自身遵守准则，还要积极引导社区成员，及时处理违规行为的举报，公平、公正地解决社区争议。
   Responsibility of contributors and maintainers: In addition to abiding by the Code themselves, they should actively guide community members, timely handle reports of violations, and resolve community disputes fairly and impartially.
3. **项目核心团队的责任**：负责本准则的解释、更新与执行，建立违规行为的处理机制，保障准则的有效落地，同时根据社区发展动态修订准则。
   Responsibility of the project core team: Be responsible for the interpretation, update and implementation of this Code, establish a handling mechanism for violations, ensure the effective implementation of the Code, and revise the Code according to the dynamic development of the community.

## 举报途径与处理流程 / Reporting Channels and Handling Process
### 1. 举报途径 / Reporting Channels
所有社区成员发现违反本准则的行为，可通过以下**官方渠道**实名/匿名举报，举报内容需客观、真实，提供相关证据（截图、聊天记录、链接等）：
- GitHub Issues：提交【行为准则举报】类Issue，标注违规行为、涉事人员、相关证据；
- 项目核心沟通渠道：Discord官方服务器的#admin-report频道。

All community members who find behaviors in violation of this Code may report them real-name/ anonymously through the following **official channels**. The reported content must be objective and true, with relevant evidence (screenshots, chat records, links, etc.):
- GitHub Issues: Submit an Issue of the type [Code of Conduct Report], marking the violation, the involved personnel, and relevant evidence;
- Project core communication channel: #admin-report channel in the official Discord server.

### 2. 处理流程 / Handling Process
1. **接收与核实**：核心维护团队在24小时内接收举报，对证据进行核实，确认是否构成违规；
2. **沟通与处理**：对违规者进行一对一沟通，提出整改要求；根据违规情节轻重，采取相应的处理措施；
3. **结果公示**：处理结果将在社区内进行公示（隐去隐私信息），接受社区监督；
4. **申诉渠道**：违规者对处理结果有异议，可通过GitHub Discussions提交申诉，核心维护团队将重新复核并给出最终结果。

1. Receipt and verification: The core maintenance team receives the report within 24 hours, verifies the evidence, and confirms whether a violation has been committed;
2. Communication and handling: Conduct one-on-one communication with the violator, put forward rectification requirements; take corresponding handling measures according to the severity of the violation;
3. Result announcement: The handling result will be announced in the community (hiding private information) and subject to community supervision;
4. Appeal channel: If the violator has an objection to the handling result, he/she may submit an appeal through GitHub Discussions, and the core maintenance team will review it again and give the final result.

### 3. 分级处理措施 / Graded Handling Measures
根据违规行为的情节轻重、影响范围与整改态度，采取以下分级处理措施，所有处理结果均由核心维护团队集体决策：
1. **口头提醒**：轻微违规，首次发生，由维护者进行善意提醒，要求立即整改；
2. **书面警告**：多次轻微违规或单次一般违规，发布书面警告，记录违规行为，限期整改；
3. **临时限制参与**：严重违规或拒不整改，临时限制其社区参与权限（1-30天），包括禁止提交PR/Issue、禁止在沟通渠道发言；
4. **永久禁止参与**：恶性违规（如人身攻击、知识产权侵权、恶意破坏仓库）、多次严重违规或整改无效，永久禁止参与项目所有协作活动，移除相关社区权限。

According to the severity of the violation, the scope of influence and the attitude of rectification, the following graded handling measures will be adopted, and all handling results will be collectively decided by the core maintenance team:
1. Verbal reminder: Minor violation, first occurrence, the maintainer gives a kind reminder and requires immediate rectification;
2. Written warning: Multiple minor violations or a single general violation, a written warning is issued, the violation is recorded, and rectification is required within a time limit;
3. Temporary restriction of participation: Severe violation or refusal to rectify, temporarily restrict its community participation rights (1-30 days), including prohibiting submission of PR/Issues and speaking in communication channels;
4. Permanent ban from participation: Malicious violations (such as personal attack, intellectual property infringement, malicious damage to the repository), multiple serious violations or ineffective rectification, permanently ban from participating in all collaborative activities of the project and remove relevant community permissions.

## 准则的更新与解释 / Update and Interpretation
1. **准则更新**：本行为准则将根据项目发展、社区反馈与实际需求进行修订，所有修订需经核心维护团队讨论并通过社区公示（公示期≥7天），无重大异议后正式生效；
2. **准则解释**：本行为准则的最终解释权归「红尘灵境」项目核心维护团队所有；
3. **地域适配**：本准则兼顾全球不同地区的文化与法律要求，若与当地法律法规冲突，以当地法律法规为准。

1. Code update: This Code of Conduct will be revised according to project development, community feedback and actual needs. All revisions must be discussed by the core maintenance team and announced to the community (public notice period ≥7 days), and take effect officially after no major objections;
2. Code interpretation: The final right of interpretation of this Code of Conduct belongs to the core maintenance team of the Hongchen Lingjing project;
3. Regional adaptation: This Code takes into account the cultural and legal requirements of different regions around the world. If it conflicts with local laws and regulations, the local laws and regulations shall prevail.
